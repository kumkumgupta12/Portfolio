About me

Hi, I’m Kumkum, a technology enthusiast currently working as a Data Scientist at Tiger Analytics. My journey in tech began at IIT Bhubaneswar, where I explored the intersection of Civil Engineering and Artificial Intelligence, integrating AI-driven solutions into traditional engineering problems. This experience not only deepened my problem-solving skills but also reinforced my ability to adapt and learn across diverse fields.

I am particularly interested in developing end-to-end machine learning and deep learning solutions, focusing on areas like computer vision, transformers and exploring Natural Language Processing (NLP), and large-scale AI systems. I enjoy understanding the entire lifecycle of AI models—from data processing and model training to deployment and real-world integration.

Beyond AI, I have hands-on experience in web development and a strong inclination toward building practical, scalable tech solutions. I see technology as a constantly evolving space, and I’m always eager to explore new advancements, refine my expertise, and contribute to meaningful innovations.

My goal is to contribute to cutting-edge AI research and develop AI-driven solutions that ease everyday life.
